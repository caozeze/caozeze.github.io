FineBI E-commerce Platform Consumer Data Visualisation
Apr 29, 2022

1. FineBI E-commerce Platform Consumer Data Visualisation

1.1. Project Background

Comprehending and interpreting data is crucial in the world of business. With the help of Business Intelligence (BI) tools, we aim to analyse data from a business context, providing in-depth insights and bringing forth practical commercial value. We employed techniques such as the RFM Model, Shopping Basket Analysis, and Pareto (ABC) principles to create visualisations for the management personnel in the sales and operations departments, with the goal of promoting “data-empowered decision-making”.

Project Link: FineBI E-commerce Platform Consumer Data Visualisation
Project Timeline: 04/16/2022 - 04/29/2022
Role: E-commerce Data Analyst
Tools Used: Python (Pandas, Numpy, FineBI, etc.)
Source Data: The primary data source of our project is the business performance data, which includes sales and operational metrics. This data set contains valuable information that aids in understanding the overall business performance.
Responsibilities: Project Management / Article Production / Application of RFM Model / Part of FineBI Charts Design

1.2. Project Process

  1. Employed Pandas for data processing, handling null values, missing values, duplicated values and insignificant columns
  2. Deployed Dashboard for visualisation of Model(RFM/Pareto(ABC)/Basket Analysis and etc.) results
  3. presented conclusions and suggestions based on the analysis results

1.3. Project Value

  1. Conducted a multi-dimensional breakdown of the overall data to identify leading products and related combination product categories to further increase sales revenue
  2. Utilised time-series and RFM model for precision operations on users, laying the groundwork for subsequent experiments (e.g., labelling price-sensitive users)

2. Detailed Report

2.1. Analysis Objectives

Our data analysis objectives mainly include the following aspects:

  1. Seasonal and geographical trends in sales.
  2. User activity patterns and customer retention rates.
  3. Gaining insights to drive sales strategies and improve operational efficiency.

2.2. Analysis Results and Suggestions

Our data analysis reveals the following key findings, based on which we propose some marketing strategy suggestions:

  1. Sales were higher in the third quarter, and some regions like Shanghai, Beijing, and Guangdong showed higher sales numbers.
  2. There was a trend of increased user activity between July and August, and the overall customer retention rate was on an upward trajectory.
  3. Our key retained customers significantly contributed to consumption.

2.3. Conclusion

Our journey of exploring BI tools, understanding data analysis models, building an efficient dashboard, and drawing commercial insights has been enlightening.
